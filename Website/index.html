Signless

Bringing Audio to Life with Sign Language

About Signless

Signless is a cutting-edge application designed to make audio content accessible to the deaf and hard of hearing community by converting spoken language into sign language. Utilizing advanced AI and machine learning technologies, Signless captures audio inputs and translates them into accurate sign language gestures displayed through an avatar.

Features

  • Real-time audio to sign language translation
  • Support for multiple languages (Spanish, Portuguese, etc.)
  • Intuitive user interface for easy operation
  • High accuracy translations powered by AI
